Our Rides and Attractions

Grand Carousel

The first double decked carousel in the country is a sight to behold. It has a capacity of 72 people, and is a truly worthy center piece all-family ride in the park Guest must be above 42 inches to ride alone Children 30 inches to 42 inches with Responsible Mature Person.

Surf Dance

This platform thrill ride captures the tumult of riding the waves with every twist and turn and lift and drop! For Guests 48 inches and above.

Jungle Splash

Take a log boat ride on a white water river that snakes through the heart of the Jungle and climaxes in a thrilling drop. Not for those afraid to get a little wet! For Guest 54 inches and Above.

Giant Star Wheel

The Philippines Tallest Ferris wheel offers a majestic view of Roxas Boulevard and the Manila Bay as well as the expanse of the reclaimed landscape. It stands 60 meters high with 32 gondolas that can accommodate 6 passengers each. Children 34 inches to 48 inches must be accompanied by a Responsible Mature Person.

Kiddie Bumper Cars

Let your kids experience the fun and excitement of driving without the hassle of the city's heavy traffic Children must be 42 inches to 48 inches only.

Quack Quack

This seven-meter revolving kiddie ride has six duck tubs each seating up to four Children must be 34 inches to 43 inches only.

Snow World

Who says we can't have a White Christmas in the Philippines? Experience snow and the biggest icy and snowy slide in the country REQUIRES SEPARATE FEE Guests must be at least 48 inches and not more than 72 inches to use the slide Children 34 inches to 48 inches with Responsible Mature Person.

Star Flyer

The only inverted roller coaster in the Philippines makes the rider know how it feels like to fly with your feet dangling in every loop and turn at great speed. For Guest 54 inches to 74 inches only.

Kid's Playland

A showcase of playground favorites on a padded surface. Kids can release all that energy and have hours of great indoor fun. Children must be 30 inches to 48 inches only with Responsible Mature Person.

Time Warp Adventure

Time Travel is now possible here at Star City! Time Warp Adventure is an EDUTAINMENT attraction that walks you through the pages of history. Children must be at least 30 inches to enter Children 30 inches to 42 inches must be accompanied by a Responsible Mature Person.
